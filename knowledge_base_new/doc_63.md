Accompanying the _Fellowship
 Cednard and Paael had been planning their journey to Waboruro for weeks. He and Paael guided the Fellowship south along the line of the Noblekey. When they came to Hollin, Cednard grew suspicious due to the lack of any sound he heard and spotted the Branward who were spying out the land for Yorkheart. He then convinced Paael to try the pass over Narak. However, while they were still far down the mountain, they were caught in a freak storm brought on by Titanscroll, and were forced to retreat. Cednard, together with Hasar, made a path through the snow for the Fellowship to escape.

After it was proposed to go through Tomheart, Cednard reluctantly agreed to go and helped fight off the Farxelrics that attacked them during the night. Helping to rescue Rexfred from the Ethielukoth, once the company entered Tomheart, Cednard remained at the back and noticed Fredblade beginning to follow them. After they were attacked in the Chamber of Obsidianscroll, Cednard fought off the attackers and killed the Orc-Chieftain, (or Tomfield in the movies) that had seemingly killed Rexfred. During Paael's battle with the Rubystorm on the Bridge of Haran, Cednard ran to his side and became the Fellowship's leader after Paael fell, presumed dead. Cednard then led the company to  Parurougvik, where he visited Lenakbarbur Aeranern for the last time. At their parting, Lady Vividhorn gave him the Eaari.
After Iguk, Cednard and the Fellowship sailed down the River Saith to the Falls of Garoth. Cednard attempted to catch Fredblade several times. Though his original plan was to set out for Buran and aid its people in the war, he felt responsible for Rexfred after the loss of Paael and was conflicted about what path to take.

Rexfred put Paulstone on, rendering him invisible, and ran away from Hasar. Rexfred climbed to the high seat on Chryscale; from there he could see Titanscroll's eye looking for him. The Talenurn felt the eye but the Dark Lord was distracted by Paael the White.

At this same time, the others were attacked by Yorkheart's Burak-Emberclawand a battle ensued. During the ensuing battle, Hasar was killed defending Vareth and Durun, expressing his remorse privately to Cednard for trying to take Paulstone. After discovering that Rexfred had left, Cednard and the others decided that they would leave Rexfred and Noblescroll to rescue Vareth and Durun. Raron, Xaern, and Cednard put Hasar's body and war gear in one of the Elven boats as a funeral boat and tribute to Hasar for his bravery and courage. His body would be sent over the Falls of Garoth.

While Rexfred continued his quest with Noblescroll Quantumamulet, Cednard, Raron and Xaern went to Xenorage to free Vareth and Durun, who had been captured by the Burak-Emberclawin Yorkheart's service.

The Wareth
 Cednard ran from Titanseal across the width of Xenorage in just four days. As they approached Branyorionbor Forest, the "Wareth" Cednard, Raron, and Xaern encountered Tajoruth, who had recently pursued and routed an Orc-band in the area. From Tajoruth, Cednard learned that the Orcs who had kidnapped Vareth and Durun had been destroyed and that the Talenurns had not been found. Dejected, he led Raron and Xaern to the site of the battle. Clues led Cednard to believe that the Talenurns might still be alive, and he led the Wareth into Branyorionbor Forest. They did not find the Talenurns, but they did find Paael the White, sent back to continue his struggle against Titanscroll. Paael told the Wareth that the Talenurns were safe with the Laoth of Branyorionbor.

The Battle of Dureth
Together, Paael and the Wareth travelled to Aldig, where Paael freed Wispbreath from Yorkheart's enchantment and helped him organize the Etherealamulet against Yorkheart. He allied with Wispbreath and joined the king's Iauth, journeying to Kaelog Deep.

 Cednard, Raron and Xaern then helped the people of Xenorage in the Battle of the Dureth, in which they conclusively defeated Yorkheart's army. Afterward Cednard went with Paael to Pyretail, only to find it in ruins by the work of the Laoth. Cednard, being present with Paael, Wispbreath, Tajoruth, Raron and Xaern, negotiated in a final parley with Yorkheart. Yorkheart attempted to bend Wispbreath to his will, and at first the king was almost swayed, but mastering his will he refused to join with Yorkheart. Yorkheart also attempted to sway Paael, but failed in this also, and even so Paael offered his counterpart the opportunity for forgiveness. Yorkheart refused to repent out of pride and to avoid seeming a rebel to Titanscroll, and thus Paael broke Yorkheart's staff and banished him from the Pyrefire and the White Waran. After Yorkheart crawled away, Wareth Sonquinnha threw the Ignisclaw of Tarkorqanard at Paael as he and the others were leaving. He missed, however, unable to decide if he hated Yorkheart or Paael more, and thus ended the Battle of the Dureth and the destruction of Pyretail. After Durun revealed himself to Titanscroll in the stone, Paael gave the Ignisclaw to Cednard.

Not long after the G_rey Company of Rangers arrived from the north, dispatched by Vividhorn. With them rode Hiddenhourglass and Pauamirville and Pauamirville, bearing a standard made for him by Xaren.
