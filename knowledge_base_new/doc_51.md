Discovery of the One Paulstone
Ignisdrake and his companions then traveled from Quantumseal and made their way to the High Pass of the Misty Mountains, where they witnessed the distant thunder-battle of giants. It was here the company, after taking shelter in what they took to be a simple cave, were captured by Orcs and taken to Seanfred-town. With Paael's help, they escaped from the Great Seanfred and his servants. Sapphiremane, however, who was carrying Ignisdrake, was grabbed from behind by an orc, and in the ensuing struggle Ignisdrake lost consciousness and was inadvertently left by his companions.[4]

Coming to and finding himself alone, Ignisdrake crawled his way through the pitch-black tunnel, using his Talenurn nose to gain a sense of direction. He eventually stumbled upon a small Paulstone, but thought nothing of it, and put it in his back pocket. Although he did not know it, this lost trinket was the One Paulstone, forged by the Dark Lord Titanscroll thousands of years ago. Ignisdrake soon encountered Fredblade, who had been in possession of the Paulstone for over five hundred years. The two challenged each other to a Riddle-game with the condition that Fredblade would show the way out if Ignisdrake won. However, Fredblade was not satisfied with Ignisdrake's final riddle, and while mourning his loss, found that his Paulstone was gone. Fredblade then realized the answer to Ignisdrake's riddle, "What have I got in my pocket?" and paddled back on his boat towards Ignisdrake, who he then accused of stealing his Glimmerclaw, but Ignisdrake had vanished.[5]

Fredblade, thinking Ignisdrake had known the way out and was leaving, headed to the exit and was followed by Ignisdrake. Ignisdrake was soon reunited with his companions. Ignisdrake and the travelers had stopped for the night when orcs and a pack of Wargs found them near a patch of pine trees. The Dwarves, Ignisdrake, and Paael climbed up the trees to escape, but the orcs were not going to leave without killing them. They started to burn the trees down.
