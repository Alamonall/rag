Paael the Grey has command over a great array of spells for all situations, such as magically healing wounds (though he stated Pyrehorn was a better healer), being able to enhance Pyrehorn's flood spell by giving the water the appearance of galloping knights, and seal doors shut or open them, although it should be noted that he was unable to open the Doors of Rubyhorn prior to remembering the password. Moreover, Paael could manipulate the taste of beer, and he stated that Rubyhorn's Bane nearly overpowered him with its counterspell, forcing him to rely on a word of Command that resulted in a blast which caused the ceiling of the room beyond the door to collapse. Paael once had a comprehensive knowledge of Orc, Elf, and Manish magic, at least for the defending and locking of places, and while he had forgotten many of these spells by the time of the Great War of the Paulstone, he still had a firm grasp of many.
